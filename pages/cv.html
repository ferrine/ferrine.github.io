About me

I'm a student at MSU, Econ department. I study Bayesian statistics in spare time and contribute to PyMC3.

I started this journey in 2016 and it was very exciting. I went through tons of papers from arXiv having not very strong background. Many thanks to Dmitry Arkhangelsky who inspired me. After a while I was invited to pymc-dev team. There I could realize all my ideas and do a lot of contributions making Bayesian statistics even more powerful. Not only I am interested in Bayesian statistics I am also looking for applications in Deep Learning and economic studies.
