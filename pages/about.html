Maxim Kochurov

Fields of interest: Bayesian statistics, variational inference, machine learning, causal inference

About me

I started the journey to machine learning in far 2016. I was a 2d year bachelor student at MSU, Econ department. In my spare time (I tried to have a lot of it) I learned programming and some basis of data science. It was a random idea that came to me in summer. That appeared to be very interesting.

Many thanks to Dmitry Arkhangelsky who inspired me. He held a course on applied econometrics giving fundamentals of Bayesian reasoning and modelling. The course was tough with lots of math and practice. After it I started reading arXiv papers on Bayesian deep learning.

While being on an internship @ Yandex I was having fun with variational inference reimplementing one paper. It helped me to get in related stuff deeper and have more practice in coding. Yeah, good time, summer after 2d school year, I found fancy things, interesting people at ods.ai. People were super cool and they also shared their experience, it helped to get a mode complete view on what's going on in the field.

All this Bayesian stuff leads not to Rome but to PyMC3. That meeting seemed to be unavoidable. I was looking for ADVI algo implementations, these guys implemented one on top of Theano. I was not satisfied with it and decided to refactor. It passed for 1.5 years, I was rewriting it, adding some novel algorithms, generalising the implementation. OSS experience is the one I value the most. It gives you a lot: people, development and collaboration skills, sense of responsibility. It also teaches you to always have a roadmap, plan longer-term. I still maintain variational inference in PyMC3 that is now in a mature stage. We also plan to build PyMC4.

Time passed, I had a Data Science related internship @ Yandex again. That was a consulting experience, it was valuable, but I was getting more and more biased into research. Right after the internship I was invited to BayesGroup.

I got myself satisfied with what I do, finally.
