In Search Of The Holy Posterior

май 05, 2017

Accepted to GSoC

Hurray!

Yesterday I got an email from NumFOCUS with congratulations. They said that I had been accepted to GSoC. So summer is going to be full of adventures. I'd like say many thanks to my mentors, Taku Yoshioka and Thomas Wiecki, for choosing me.

A lot of work is going to be done. I'm very excited with Bayesian stuff and what's the best is the feeling that you collaborate with pymc-dev team from all over the word. That you work on something that not only is needed by yourself but can solve other's people problems. That what you do can be easily scaled up with GPU and do bayesian inference on really big data.

We are about the time where risk weighted decisions can be done in Deep Learning. The only thing is needed is to reinterpret deterministic model as probabilistic model that predicts not a single point but a distribution. There are few libraries that allow to do so (Edward and PyMC3). The main idea behind my proposal is to make VI closer to the final user, data scientist, that constructs models and solves business problems. That's why I've started Gelato project. It is in not in a release stage yet, but I have a strong desire to change it.

To conclude I'm going to have best summer ever!
