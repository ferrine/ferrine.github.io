# Recent works
There is a bunch of recent works about causal inference as wall as fundamental ones:

1. [The Book of Why](https://www.amazon.com/Book-Why-Science-Cause-Effect/dp/046509760X)
2. [Multiple Causal Inference with Latent Confounding](https://arxiv.org/abs/1805.08273) (2018)
3. [The Blessings of Multiple Causes](https://arxiv.org/abs/1805.06826) (2018)
4. [Implicit Causal Models for Genome-wide Association Studies](https://arxiv.org/abs/1710.10742) (2017)
5. [Causal Effect Inference with Deep Latent-Variable Models](https://arxiv.org/abs/1705.08821) (2017)

These works cover major challenges in capturing causality structure
